Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating smart machines.  
AI systems can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.  
Machine learning is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data.  
Natural language processing allows computers to understand human language.  
AI is used in various industries, including healthcare and finance.  
Self-driving cars rely heavily on AI technologies for navigation.  
Voice assistants like Siri and Alexa are powered by AI algorithms.  
AI can help detect fraud in banking transactions.  
Robotics often incorporates AI for autonomous decision-making.  
Ethical considerations are important in the development of AI systems.
